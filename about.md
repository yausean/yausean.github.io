## About Sean Yau

Sean Yau is a Senior Business Manager at Standard Chartered Bank, where he oversees Financial Markets operations across Africa, the Middle East, ASEAN, and South Asia.

With more than 16 years of experience in banking and finance, he has led work across Finance, FP&A, Treasury Data Analytics, and FX Product Control. His career has included roles at Standard Chartered Bank, Barclays Bank, PricewaterhouseCoopers, and Ernst & Young.

Sean holds a Master of IT in Business from Singapore Management University, where he deepened his interest in technology-enabled finance, analytics, and process digitization. His technical toolkit includes R, SQL, Python, and Tableau.

He is currently pursuing doctoral research on AI and financial governance, focused on identifying risks introduced by AI in finance and strengthening standards that support stability, integrity, and accountability.

Outside his professional work, Sean volunteers in charity-driven food distribution efforts and has taught Python programming to underprivileged children. He also enjoys literature, history, sports, and reading with his daughter.
